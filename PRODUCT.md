# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Young adults (18–35) in Ghana and across Africa who want to send and receive money without friction. They live on their phones, transact daily, and are tired of apps that feel foreign-made or untrustworthy. Secondary users are developers and businesses building payment integrations on top of Aza.

## Product Purpose

Aza is a mobile-first fintech platform for instant money transfer, peer chat, QR payments, and a mini-app hub — all secured with bank-level encryption. It exists to make money movement as fast and frictionless as messaging someone. Success looks like the app someone opens first when a bill needs splitting or a friend needs paying.

## Brand Personality

Sharp. Minimal. Fast. The brand communicates through precision and restraint — never cluttered, never showy. Every element earns its place. The aesthetic is dark and premium without being cold; the lime accent carries warmth and energy.

## Anti-references

- **Wave / Chipper Money**: Navy + gold African fintech template — too common, too expected.
- **Cash App**: Plain black + green, too minimal, no personality or cultural resonance.
- **Generic SaaS**: Cream background, eyebrow labels on every section, identical icon-above-text card grids — AI scaffolding, not brand.
- **Crypto / Web3**: Purple gradients, heavy glow effects, glassy cards — wrong register entirely.

## Design Principles

1. **Conviction at first glance.** The visitor should feel "this is the one" before reading a word. Visual confidence is not optional.
2. **Speed is the product.** Every animation, transition, and layout choice should communicate that Aza moves fast.
3. **Dark as default voice.** The dark palette is not decoration — it signals seriousness, security, and premium craft. Never undermine it with muddy neutrals.
4. **Lime carries the energy.** The #B7EE7A accent is the single most expressive element in the system. Use it surgically — every appearance should feel intentional, not scattered.
5. **Show, don't describe.** Product screenshots, UI mockups, and real interactions beat any amount of copy. If it can be shown, show it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as the floor. prefers-reduced-motion respected throughout — every animation has an instant-reveal fallback. Lime (#B7EE7A) on dark green (#174717) passes 4.5:1 for body text. All interactive elements have visible focus rings.
